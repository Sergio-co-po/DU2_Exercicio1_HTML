What are monkeys?

With the term monkey we usually refer colloquially to any of the 200 knownspecies of primates with tails, classifiable into platyrrhines (New World monkeys) or cercopithecoids (Old World monkeys), given that they can lead very different life patterns, depending on the habitat and the species.

Characteristics of monkeys

  • They are mammals, endowed with four prehensile limbs and a tail,
  • They have:
    • Five-fingered hands
    • Clawless
    • Equipped with an opposable thumb
  • They have dentition similar to that of humans:
    1. Three molars
    2. Two incisors
    3. One tusk on each side of the mouth
  • They have a great visual capacity
